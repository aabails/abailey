Using the ReMarkable 2 for Clinical Counselling Notes

By Aaron Bailey, Counsellor

In my counselling practice, keeping clear and structured notes is essential. Over time, I’ve found that the ReMarkable 2 tablet provides the perfect balance: it keeps me paper-free while still giving me the focus and tactile experience of writing by hand. Unlike a laptop or phone, the ReMarkable minimizes distractions and helps me stay present with clients during sessions.

To make the most of this workflow, I designed a Clinical Counselling Notes Template specifically for my ReMarkable. It allows me to quickly capture key points, structure my observations, and plan for future sessions in a way that is professional and efficient.

🖊️ Why ReMarkable for Counselling Notes?

  • Focus – no notifications or distractions during sessions.
  • Organization – all notes are stored digitally in one place.
  • Security – files can be synced securely and backed up.
  • Handwriting – natural feel makes note-taking intuitive.

📋 The Clinical Counselling Notes Template

This template is designed to guide my note-taking process during sessions. It includes space for:

  • Main Points of Discussion – a quick summary of core topics.
  • Session Notes & Observations – space for free-form clinical notes.
  • Safeguarding Concerns – clear section for any immediate risks.
  • Resources & Techniques Used – documenting strategies applied.
  • Post-Session Notes – reflections after the session ends.
  • Homework & Next Session Plan – helping track continuity of care.
