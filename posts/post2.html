5 Ways Nature Helps with Mental Health

Spending time outdoors has been shown to reduce stress and boost mood. Here are five ways nature can support mental health:

  1. Exposure to sunlight helps regulate sleep and mood.
  2. Being outdoors encourages physical activity.
  3. Green spaces lower stress hormone levels.
  4. Time in nature supports mindfulness and presence.
  5. Outdoor activities can foster connection with others.
