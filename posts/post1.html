How to Support Teens with Anxiety

Anxiety is one of the most common challenges teens face. Whether it shows up in school, friendships, or daily life, support from parents and caregivers can make a huge difference. Here are some practical tips:

  • Listen without judgment.
  • Encourage routines and healthy habits.
  • Validate feelings while offering reassurance.

Sometimes, professional support can help teens develop coping strategies and feel less alone.
