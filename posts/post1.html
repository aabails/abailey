Free Counselling Templates for ReMarkable

By Aaron Bailey, Counsellor

Over the years I’ve developed a series of counselling templates to help streamline sessions, track progress, and make planning easier. These forms were created with high school students in mind, but they’re flexible enough for many different counselling contexts.

I’m sharing them here for free as downloadable files that can be imported directly to your ReMarkable tablet or used as PDFs.

📝 Initial Counselling Session Notes

A structured intake form designed for first sessions. It includes presenting concerns, scaling questions, risk screening, strengths, and academic/future orientation prompts.

📥 Download Initial Session Notes (PDF)

🔎 Quick Check-in Notes

A short form for regular follow-up sessions. Includes quick scaling questions, safety check, and space for observations.

📥 Download Quick Check-in Notes (PDF)

📚 Counselling Notes

A general-purpose counselling notes template for documenting main topics, concerns, strategies, and next steps.

📥 Download Counselling Notes (PDF)

📅 Course Scheduling Form

A simple timetable draft form that helps students and counsellors plan courses across semesters.

📥 Download Course Scheduling Form (PDF)

🤝 ICM Meeting Notes

A structured Integrated Case Management (ICM) meeting template. Includes attendee list, strengths, challenges, and action items.

📥 Download ICM Meeting Notes (PDF)

🎯 IEP Meeting Notes

A comprehensive Individual Education Plan (IEP) meeting template with supports, strategies, goals, and action items.

How to Use These Templates

- Download the PDFs and upload them to your ReMarkable tablet, or print them if you prefer.
- Use the files as reusable templates to save time and keep session records organized.
- Adapt or customize them to fit your practice.

I hope these tools make your counselling work a little smoother. Feel free to share this page with colleagues who may also benefit.
