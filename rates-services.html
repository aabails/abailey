FAQ

Common questions about fees & payment

A few answers to questions I'm commonly asked before getting started.

Do you offer sliding-scale fees?

Yes. I reserve a limited number of reduced-fee appointments for youth and families experiencing financial hardship. If cost is a concern, please mention it during your free consultation.

Which payment methods do you accept?

I accept cash, debit, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Interac e-Transfer. Payment is collected at the beginning of each session and a receipt is emailed the same day.

Do you direct bill insurance?

Yes. Direct billing is available for Pacific Blue Cross and many other insurers. If direct billing isn't available for your plan, I'll email a receipt so you can submit it for reimbursement.

Will my insurance cover counselling?

Many extended-health plans cover Registered Clinical Counsellors (RCCs). Coverage varies by provider and plan, so it's a good idea to check your benefits before your first appointment.

What is your cancellation policy?

Appointments cancelled with more than 24 hours' notice are free of charge. Late cancellations or missed appointments may be charged the full session fee.

Can sessions be online or outdoors?

Yes. Sessions can take place in person, online, by phone, or as walk-and-talk counselling, depending on your preferences and what feels most comfortable.

Still have questions about fit or cost?

A free 15-minute consultation is the best place to ask questions, discuss your situation, and see if we're a good fit—no pressure, no obligation.

Session Format

The above sessions can be in-person, online, or by phone. In-person can take the form of a session in my office or going for a walk in nature, depending on the client's comfort level. If the sessions runs a bit longer, there will be no extra charge.

Payment Methods

I accept cash, debit, credit, or Interac e-Transfer and collect fees at the beginning of each session. A receipt will be issued by email the same day. Depending on your plan direct billing to Pacific Blue Cross and other insurers available, client will be responsible for any unpaid amounts.

Insurance Coverage & Benefits

Services may be covered in full or in part by your health insurance or employee benefit plan. Please check your coverage carefully and know:

  • Do I have mental health insurance benefits?
  • Does my insurance cover a Registered Clinical Counsellor?
  • What is my deductible and has it been met?
  • How many sessions per year does my health insurance cover?
  • What is the coverage amount per therapy session?
  • Is approval required from my primary care physician?
  • Does my provider support direct billing for counselling services?

Cancellation Policy

If you do not show up for your scheduled therapy appointment, and you have not provided notification at least 24 hours in advance, you may be required to pay the full cost of the session.
