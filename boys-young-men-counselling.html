Common Questions

A few questions parents and young men often ask.

What if I don't really like talking?

That's okay. Counselling doesn't begin with deep conversations. We build trust first, and we'll go at your pace.

My son says he's "fine." Should I still reach out?

Many boys struggle to put difficult feelings into words. Changes in mood, motivation, anger, sleep, or school can all be signs that some support could help.

Do you work with teens and young adults?

Yes. I work with youth (10+) and young adults navigating school, relationships, confidence, anxiety, ADHD, identity, and life's many transitions.

Will parents be involved?

That depends on age and what feels helpful. I value privacy while also working collaboratively with families whenever appropriate.

Do we have to know what's wrong before starting?

Not at all. Sometimes the first step is simply making sense of what's been feeling different. We can figure that out together.
