Some conversations come easier while walking. Outdoor sessions combine the same client-centred counselling you would receive in the office with the calming pace of nature. WHY WALK & TALK

A different environment can create different conversations.

Walking naturally reduces the intensity of face-to-face conversation while still allowing meaningful therapeutic work. Many clients living with anxiety, ADHD, OCD, stress, grief, burnout or life transitions find the outdoor environment helps them settle into the session more quickly.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions about outdoor counselling

Where do we meet?

Quiet accessible trails around Brentwood Bay selected for privacy.

Do I need hiking experience?

No. Sessions are gentle walks at your pace.

What if someone passes us?

We simply pause until privacy returns.

Can teens participate?

Absolutely. Many young people find walking helps them feel more comfortable.

What happens if the weather changes?

We can continue, move indoors, or switch online.
