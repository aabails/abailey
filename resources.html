If you need help now

Vancouver Island Crisis Line

Please call if you're experiencing a mental health crisis or are worried about someone else. They offer immediate support and can connect you with additional local services. Phone, text, and chat options are available.

1-888-494-3888 vicrisis.ca
Also available across Canada: Call or text 988 for suicide crisis support, 24/7.
