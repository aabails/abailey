Youth & Young Adult Counselling in Brentwood Bay (Greater Victoria)

Book a Free 15-min Consult Learn about Teen Counselling

I am dedicated to guiding young people through life's transitions and challenges with empathy and understanding. My mission is to create a supportive space where teens and young adults can explore their thoughts, feelings, and goals with a compassionate and skilled therapist.

Whether you’re navigating the complexities of adolescence, dealing with stress or anxiety, or seeking to build resilience and self-confidence, I offer personalized therapy tailored to your unique needs.

My trauma-informed, solution-focused approach recognizes that clients are the experts of their own lives. Together, we identify strengths and solutions that may not yet be fully visible.
